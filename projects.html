Our Projects

At Century Cement, we take pride in our involvement in various prestigious projects throughout Uganda. Our high-quality cement products have been instrumental in the successful completion of numerous construction ventures. Century Cement's high-quality cement can be used to construct a wide range of projects across various sectors:

Residential Buildings

Century Cement is ideal for constructing individual homes, apartment complexes, gated communities, and affordable housing projects.

Commercial Buildings

It is suitable for constructing office buildings, shopping malls, hotels, restaurants, and other commercial establishments.

Infrastructure Projects

Century Cement can be used in the construction of bridges, flyovers, highways, roads, and railway projects.

Industrial Facilities

It is used for constructing factories, warehouses, manufacturing units, and industrial parks.

Institutional Buildings

Century Cement is suitable for schools, colleges, universities, hospitals, and government buildings.

Dams and Water Management Projects

It can be used in the construction of dams, reservoirs, water treatment plants, and irrigation canals.
